About Lowell Project Code

About Us

We are Lowell Project Code, a coding club at Lowell High School, San Francisco. We meet every Monday after school at 3:40. We provide beginner and experienced pathways for people of all skill levels, and anyone can join regardless of skill level.

Our Goals

Our goal is to kickstart students' interest in coding and inspire a new generation of computer programmers and leaders.
